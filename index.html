Research Interests

Wireless Communications

Next-generation wireless systems, MIMO communications, and reconfigurable intelligent surfaces (RIS).

Signal Processing

Advanced signal processing techniques for communications, sensing, and positioning.

Optimization Algorithms

Convex and non-convex optimization, distributed optimization, and resource allocation.

Machine Learning

Applications of machine learning and deep learning to wireless communications and signal processing.

Fluid Antenna Systems

Novel antenna designs and applications for fluid antenna systems in 6G communications.

Wireless Positioning

mmWave positioning, RIS-assisted positioning, and integrated sensing and communications.

Research Projects

Reconfigurable Intelligent Surface (RIS) for 6G

2022 - Present

Investigating novel applications of RIS in 6G wireless networks, including localization, sensing, and communications. Developing optimization algorithms for RIS-assisted systems.

Fluid Antenna Systems for Next-Generation Communications

2021 - Present

Exploring the potential of fluid antenna systems for enhancing spectral efficiency and reliability in wireless communications. Developing resource allocation algorithms for fluid antenna systems.

mmWave Positioning Systems

2020 - Present

Developing advanced positioning algorithms for mmWave systems with high accuracy and reliability. Investigating the integration of positioning and communications in 6G networks.
